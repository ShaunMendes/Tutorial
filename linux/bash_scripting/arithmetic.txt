Double Paranthesis:
$(( expression ))

Eg:
a=$(( 4 + 5 ))

Length Of Variable:
${#variable}

Eg:
b=4953
echo ${#b} # 4

let:
let <arithmetic expression>

Eg:
let a=5+4
let a++

let always saves a result to variable

Expr:
expr <item1> operator <item2>

Does not save result to variable

Eg:
expr 5 + 4
a=$( expr 10 - 3 )
