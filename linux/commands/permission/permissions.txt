0 – no permission
1 – execute
2 – write
3 – write and execute
4 – read
5 – read and execute
6 – read and write
7 – read, write, and execute

What about the 3 digits ‘777’?
first digit is assigned to the Owner,
second digit is assigned to the Group
third digit is assigned to the Others

chmod 775 /path/to/file
